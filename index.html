About Roblox Lookup

Roblox Lookup is a lightweight web app that helps you discover popular games, check player profiles, and quickly jump into experiences. It uses official public endpoints for thumbnails, users, presence, and votes. No login required.

  • Fast game search with fallback crawling for better coverage.
  • User search by username or ID with avatars and live status.
  • Clean interface, keyboard shortcuts, shareable links, and recent history.

Quick Guide

Find a Game: switch to Game Search, type part of the title (e.g., “doors”, “brookhaven rp”), then press Enter. Click a result to see likes, visits, and a direct play link.

Find a Player: switch to User Search, type a username (or numeric ID), and press Enter. You’ll see avatar, bio, counts, and online status.

Deep Links: You can link directly with ?q=<term>&mode=game|user. Example: ?q=brookhaven&mode=game.

Shortcuts: / focus · Enter search · G/U toggle · 1/2 tabs.

FAQ

Is this an official Roblox site?
No. This is a community tool that links to roblox.com. All trademarks belong to their respective owners.

Why can’t I find some games?
Some titles may not appear in autocomplete; the app falls back to web results and converts to universe IDs to improve coverage.

Do you store my data?
Recent searches are saved locally on your device only. No account, no tracking beyond AdSense ads which are subject to Google’s policies.

How do I report an issue?
Use the contact section below and include the query you tried and your browser.

Privacy

We don’t run our own analytics. The page displays Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve ads; you can manage ad personalization in your Google settings. Searches happen client‑side and are not sent to our server except for standard static hosting logs.
